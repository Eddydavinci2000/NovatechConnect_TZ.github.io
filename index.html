Let's INNOVATE Together

Say Hello to NOVATECH_CONNECT

Novatech Connect Foundation seeks to implement comprehensive programs that equip people with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the digital age successfully, while fostering a greater understanding of our universe.

01 Goal

Promote technology development and awareness through digital literacy education in civic participation and universe awareness in the next 5 years within 50% of Tanzania regions through enhancing educational engagement and access to innovative solutions.

02 Mission

Steward technology development, enhancing media literacy, and promoting civic participation to empower Tanzanians.

03 Vision

To become a leading force in technology education, equipping future generations with the tools and knowledge needed to contribute to a connected world.
